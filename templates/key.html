Story

KeyQuest is a mobile web application that focuses on blending reality and the virtual world in one. Players will have to physically move to various locations at their nearest park in order to find virtual chests with the assistance of a compass. The game ends upon unlocking the door using the key that was found in the last chest.

Why

Our team's vision aimed to produce an application that would make our players coexist with the real world and the virtual one on their phone. We wanted players to enjoy nearby parks without having to feel disconnected from their devices. In order to accomplish this, the team create a web app to reach as many people as possible.
